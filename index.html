Seattle Open Device Lab

Perfect your mobile experiences

Open Device Labs are a shared community resource for testing apps and websites on a variety of phones, tablets and other devices. Instead of only testing on the few devices we personally own, or shelling out for a slightly larger set of test devices, we can build a comprehensive set of devices to share.

Seattle doesn't have an ODL yet, but we will very soon. Formidable Labs is opening a new coworking site in Fremont in December. They have graciously offered to host the Seattle Open Device Lab, and we will have an open house for the ODL in December. Please sign up below if you'd like to be notified when all the details are in place. Your address will only be used to announce big changes to the Seattle ODL (such as when the first open house is scheduled).

If you have input, please add to this thread I have started on the device-lab group, ping me at @alanstearns on Twitter, or email me (my address is my last name at adobe.com).
